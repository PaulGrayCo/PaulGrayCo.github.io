Our Work

Take a look at some of the compelling visual stories we've crafted for our clients.

Digital App Campaign - Famous Malaysian Cuisine

A Video series for Tourism Malaysia (HQ). A 50 video campaign originally shot as accompanying videos for a digital guide app. These 3-5 Minute videos showcase 50 of the most recommended dishes in Malaysia, as well as the story of the Hawkers behind them.

Event Highlights Reel - Malaysian Heritage Cuisine

A Live Video & Social Media campaign for MAFS (Agriculture Ministry - Malaysia). The highlight reel showcases the event, which was hosted across 5 Countries and shown to viewers around the world on various digital platforms.

Web Series Showcase - Street Food Journeys

A web series for Tourism Malaysia (Australia). Originally launched during the pandemic, the series grew from being shot on phones & webcams to an 8 episode back-to-back series shot in Malaysia, receiving praise from viewers & stakeholders.

Narrative Documentary - Martin Yan: A Chef's Journey

Documentary shot for Martin Yan’s Asian Culinary Tour of Sydney in October 2023. A 30-minute documentary showcasing various asian cultural hotspots around sydney, interviews with culinary experts and cooking demonstrations. The documentary captured the narrative of the immigrant story in Australia through the perspective of food.
